# Sihle Finance

A mobile-friendly personal finance app for monthly budgeting, transaction tracking, investments and purchase decisions.

## Hosting with GitHub Pages

1. Open the repository **Settings**.
2. Select **Pages** under **Code and automation**.
3. Under **Build and deployment**, choose **Deploy from a branch**.
4. Select the `main` branch and `/ (root)` folder.
5. Click **Save**.

The app will be published at:

`https://sihlemnqokoyi.github.io/sihle-finance-app/`

## Installing on a phone

Open the published site on your phone. On Android, use **Install app** or **Add to Home screen**. On iPhone, use Safari's Share button and choose **Add to Home Screen**.

The app stores financial data locally in the browser. Use **Export Data** regularly to keep a backup.
